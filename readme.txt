Everything Bagel Recipe

INGREDIENTS

For the Bagels:
- 4 cups bread flour
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1.5 teaspoons salt
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 2 teaspoons instant yeast
- 1.25 cups warm water

For Boiling:
- 2 quarts water
- 2 tablespoons honey or malt syrup

Everything Topping:
- 2 tablespoons poppy seeds
- 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
- 1 tablespoon dried minced garlic
- 1 tablespoon dried minced onion
- 1 teaspoon coarse sea salt

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Mix flour, sugar, salt, oil, yeast, and warm water in a bowl until a shaggy dough forms.
2. Knead for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
3. Divide dough into 8 equal pieces and roll each into a ball.
4. Poke a hole in the center of each ball and stretch to about 2 inches wide.
5. Let bagels rest on a parchment-lined baking sheet for 20 minutes.
6. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
7. Bring water and honey to a boil in a large pot.
8. Boil bagels for 1 minute per side.
9. Mix all topping ingredients together in a shallow bowl.
10. Dip the top of each boiled bagel into the everything topping.
11. Place bagels on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
12. Let cool slightly before serving. Enjoy!
